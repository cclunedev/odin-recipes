Pizza

Description

This is a great recipe for using premade dough to make a classic pepperoni pizza. You should make sure to use high quality ingredients to ensure the end result is delicious!

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta. A person who makes pizza is known as a pizzaiolo.

Ingredients

  • 12 Inch Premade Pizza Dough
  • 1.5 Cups Fresh Tomato Sauce
  • 12 oz Shredded Mozzarella
  • 20 Slices Precut Pepperoni
  • 2 Tbps Parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 Degrees Farenheit
  2. Place the premade pizza dough onto a pizza stone
  3. Pour tomato sauce onto center of the dough. Using a laddle, spiral the sauce in an outward circle to create a smooth, even sauce base. Leave the outter 1/2 inch uncovered.
  4. Pour shredded mozzarella over the sauced dough and sprea evenly
  5. Scatter pepperoni evenly over the cheesed area of the pizza
  6. Sprinkle parsley on pizza to garnish
  7. Place the pizza in the preheated oven. Cook 15-20 minutes
